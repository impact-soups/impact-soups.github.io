# impact-soups.github.io

Website for the Impact@SOUPS workshop - a community gathering focused on creating significant, sustained societal impact through usable privacy and security research. The workshop brings together researchers to discuss pathways toward real-world change through technology design, policy development, and community programs.

- Date: August 23, 2026
- Location: Hannover, Germany
